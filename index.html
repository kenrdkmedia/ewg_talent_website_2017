The Industry Leading Staffing Service

Event Workforce Group Talent is the leading event workforce solution. We provide motivated and experienced staff (known as 'Workforcers') to events in all shapes and sizes. From staff to manage brand activations, event operations, ticketing, hospitality and merchandising crews, through to our more experienced contractors and full time candidates for ongoing roles. With over 20,000 Workforcers nationwide, we have the ability to provide not only the number of staff required but the skill sets and experience necessary to get the job done.
